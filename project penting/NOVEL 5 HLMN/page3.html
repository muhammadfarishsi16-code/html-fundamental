☯ The Eternal Reign

Legacy of the Dark Alliance

Centuries later, the symbols of The Shadowfang Pact endure — carved into ruins, whispered in forbidden prayers.

Scholars speak of their reign as an era of cruel order, yet unmatched prosperity for the loyal.

Dravengard’s dragons became myths; Serpenthara’s serpents became faiths in disguise.

Volmir’s lion adorns the crowns of warlords, and Noxvalen’s star guides explorers into uncharted night skies.

Pandemon’s creations still breathe in the dark laboratories of secret orders.

Aureath’s eagle remains a sigil of pride — and tyranny.

But above all, the Shadowfang crown waits to return, its fangs sharp, its will eternal.

Legends claim that one day, when the six banners rise again under the shadowed crown...

...the world shall once more kneel beneath the pact reborn.

And the age of light shall end — forevermore.
