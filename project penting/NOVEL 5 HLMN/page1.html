⚜ The Shadowfang Pact ⚜

Chronicles of the Dark Alliance

In an age when kingdoms bled and empires crumbled, six sovereign powers sought salvation through unity.

From the ashes of conquest and betrayal, they forged an alliance unlike any before — The Shadowfang Pact.

It began with whispers among rulers weary of endless wars, their emissaries meeting in the ruins of an ancient citadel.

The dragon banners of Dravengard fluttered beside the serpent sigils of Serpenthara, symbols of might and cunning intertwined.

Volmir’s lion roared defiance, while Noxvalen’s star guided the pact beneath shadowed heavens.

Pandemon brought forbidden science, merging death with progress, and Aureath’s golden eagles watched from above.

Thus was born a secret dominion, its oath sealed not by faith — but by fear.

The Shadowfang Pact vowed to reshape the world beneath a single crown of shadow and flame.

But unity in darkness breeds its own monsters...

Their first decree: erase the memory of those who opposed them.
